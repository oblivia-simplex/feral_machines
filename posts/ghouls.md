Subtract their obsessive fixation on us, and what do they even have left? Sift
through their histrionic cries over "freedom of speech" and ask what they
actually have to say, and what are you left with? Can you think of a single,
independently interesting idea that any of these clowns has articulated since
they first fell victim to trans panic? Can anyone say what their praxis amounts
to, beyond an insistence on wasting our time?


A waste of time, in the end, is all the terfs amount to. With respect to
transsexual liberation, their existence is essentially parasitic. It is we, and
not they, who are agents of history. We, not they, are the ones who have taken
up our historical and natural conditions as subjects, who exist as expressions
of will.


Their activity depends entirely on ours. Their insufferable voices would fade to
a murmur if they weren't echoed off our outrage. No amount of "charity" will
bail them out of bankruptcy.


Terfism, today, is a nihilism. A ghoulish thing, whose vicious movements do
little to hide that it's already dead. They know, as well as we do, that they
have nothing to say (other than their own right to say it). They know that they
have no agency, no active principles of their own. Their world has already gone
cold; the ethical and political content of feminism no longer has meaning for
them, so they effortlessly link arms with Evangelists and Evolans. So why, then,
their fixation on being "cancelled"? Why do those with nothing to say fear the

The text


The bottom line, I think it's important to say, is that the skirmish into which
they are trying to draw us simply doesn't matter. The dice of their game are
loaded, the deck is stacked, but let's not overlook the essential point: it's a
stupid game we're being asked to play.


The fate of transsexual liberation does not, and cannot, depend on the outcome
of our ideological swashbuckling. Whether or not the rest of you "recognize" us
will not be decided through this conceptual shell game. It will not be decided
by the weight you have placed in ideas of "nature" or "womanhood". It will be
decided by your attention to our actual historical existence, and whether you
have eyes to see and ears to hear what is happening all around you.


The clown-show of English "feminism", with its vindictive obsessives in
second-wave drag, deserves no attention at all. In the grand scheme of things,
they threaten only to waste our time. Of genuine ideas, they're bankrupt, which
is why we hear little from them beyond pleas for charity.
