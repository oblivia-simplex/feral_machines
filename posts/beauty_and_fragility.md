The memory of a remark made by Moebius comes back to me, every now and again:

> Drawing a man is very different from drawing a woman. With males, you can be
> looser and less precise in their depiction; small imperfections can often add
> character. Your drawing of a woman, however, must be perfect; a single
> ill-placed line can dramatically age her or make her seem annoying or ugly.
> Then, no one buys your comic!
